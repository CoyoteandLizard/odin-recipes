Fried Rice

Fried rice is a great side or even the main course.

Description

This is a very common dish in many parts of the world. Cheap, easy, and filling

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • frozen peas and carrots
  • cumin
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • Brocoli
  • Butter

Steps

  1. cook rice in rice cooker and refridgerate in shallow dish for 1 hour
  2. Heat up wok on medium heat
  3. Pour small amount of seame oil into wok
  4. When oil gets hot slowly add cooled rice, you should hear it sizzle
  5. Once all the rice is in stir heavily every 30-45 seconds
  6. After 3 or 4 stirs add the butter and make sure it's not burning
  7. Now add the cumin and keep stiring, add a little more oil or butter if it looks too dry
  8. Once the rice starts really smelling like popcorn, add the frozen veggies
  9. While the veggies are starting to thaw, turn the heat to low
  10. Now wash and chop your brocoli and add it to the wok
  11. Give everything a good stir and add soy sauce to taste
