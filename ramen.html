Ramen Noodles

Ramen noodles are my favorite quick and easy meal

Description

As with most of my recipes, there is a lot of room for creativity. Perfect for those cold days and you just want something warm and fulling.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Hot water
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Balsamic vinegar or Chinese black vinegar
  • butter or margarine
  • spices
  • can of mushrooms
  • fresh chives
